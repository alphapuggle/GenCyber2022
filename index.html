  • TBC

    Function:

    TBC (Time based Classroom) Takes the current time and opens the corresponding classroom.

    Why TBC?

    TBC's purpose is to have one webpage to open for each class. This site will open everything else you may need for that class. For example, pearsonsuccessnet for math and my.hrw.com for science.

    Known Issues

    Known issues with TBC include, but are not limited to

    • Some classes that have textbooks are not available. For these classes, like french, you could use other classes that utilize the same site. Ex. Science for French.
    • The times do not adjust for two-hour delay days, assemblys, or other non-normal schedule
    • Old versions will not update Version detection added, old versions will reprompt the firstrun setup

    Issues
